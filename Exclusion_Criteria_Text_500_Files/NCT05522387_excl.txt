Exclusion Criteria:~1. Any clinically significant abnormalities that in the opinion of the Investigator require
further investigation or treatment or may interfere with study procedures and assessments or affect patient
safety. These include but are not limited to, laboratory tests, electrocardiogram (ECG), physical examination,
or vital signs at Screening or other medical conditions (e.g., cardiac, respiratory, gastrointestinal,
psychiatric, renal disease) which are not adequately and stably controlled.~2. Unable to comply with the study
procedures and assessments.
