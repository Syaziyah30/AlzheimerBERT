Exclusion Criteria:~* Significant neurological or psychiatric disorders, other than AD, that may affect
cognition.~* Atypical clinical presentations of MCI due to AD or mild dementia due to AD, such as the visual
variant of AD (including posterior cortical atrophy), frontal variant or the language variant (including
logopenic aphasia).~* Moderate and severe dementia with a Mini-Mental State Examination score (MMSE) below
20.~* Current presence of a clinically important major psychiatric disorder (e.g. major depressive disorder) as
defined by DSM-5 criteria, or symptom(s) (e.g. hallucinations) that could affect the subject's ability to
complete the study.~* History of clinically evident stroke.~* History of seizures within the last two years
prior to the screening visit.~* Myocardial infarction within the last six months prior to screening.~* History
of uncontrolled hypertension (in the opinion of the investigator) within six months prior to screening.~*
Contraindication to lumbar puncture and MRI
