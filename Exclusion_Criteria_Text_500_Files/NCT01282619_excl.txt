Exclusion Criteria:~1. Proven or clinically suspected other type of dementia such as vascular dementia,
mechanic or chemical caused dementia, vitamin B12 deficiency, hypothyroidism etc.~2. Epileptic Patient~3.
Severe liver or renal disease~4. Resting pulse less than 50~5. Mechanical intestinal obstruction patient~6.
History of stroke~7. History of angina or other severe cardiac, vascular, lung, gastrointestinal and
hematopoietic system disease~8. Cognitive damage caused by alcohol or substance abuse~9. Disable to participate
or cooperate in the protocol~10. Use of any agent for the treatment of dementia within 2 weeks of
randomization~11. Use of another investigational agent within 3 months of screening~12. Be sensitive to
Huperzine A or other ACHEIs.
