Exclusion Criteria:~1. Current medical or neurological condition that might impact cognition or performance on
cognitive assessments. (e.g., traumatic brain injury (TBI), Parkinson's disease (PD), multiple sclerosis,
etc.)~2. Inability or unwillingness of patient to undergo neuropsychological testing.~3. Advanced, severe,
progressive or unstable disease that may interfere with the safety, tolerability and study assessments, or put
the subject at special risk. (e.g., significant cardiac disease, severe renal impairment, severe hepatic
impairment, autoimmune disease, etc.)~4. History of malignancy of any organ system within the past 60 months,
that in the opinion of the investigator would impede evaluation or interpretation of subject safety or study
results.~5. Females of childbearing potential must not be pregnant.~6. Inability or unwillingness to undergo
PET Scans.~7. Inability or unwillingness to undergo MRI Scans.~8. Positive blood test for either HIV, Hepatitis
B, Hepatitis C or Syphilis~9. Positive for TSPO SNP rs6971~10. Inability or unwillingness to undergo Lumbar
Punctures.~11. Inability or unwillingness to undergo infusions.~12. Any condition, which in the opinion of the
investigator, would put the subject at undue risk or would interfere with evaluation of the investigational
product or interpretation of subject safety or study results.
