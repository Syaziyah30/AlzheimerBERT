Exclusion Criteria:~* Patients with neurodegenerative diseases other than Alzheimer's disease, such as Lewy
bodies disease, (dementia due to tiny round structures made of proteins that develop within nerve cells in the
brain), Parkinsonism, etc~* Patients with cognitive dysfunction due to cerebral damage resulting from a lack of
oxygen, a brain injury, etc~* Patients with multi-infarct dementia (brought on by a series of strokes) or
active cerebrovascular disease~* Patients with clinically significant cardiovascular disease~* Patients
currently taking drugs such as a cholinesterase inhibitors, which improve cerebral circulation/metabolism
