Exclusion Criteria:~Any contraindication to clinical lumbar puncture including increased intracranial pressure,
posterior fossa mass, bleeding diathesis, use of antiplatelet medications other than aspirin, use of any
anticoagulant~Severe cerebrovascular disease~History of large territory stroke~Allergy or sensitivity to
B-vitamins or nicotinic acid~History of elevated liver function tests (ALT/AST > 2x the upper limit of normal)
or known liver disease~Current consumption of Vitamin B3 (any form, including nicotinic acid) - including
multivitamins and energy drinks. Participants taking a supplement containing Niacin must washout for 4 weeks
prior to screening to participate.~Renal impairment of Stage 2 or greater
