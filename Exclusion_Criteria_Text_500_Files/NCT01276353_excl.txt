Exclusion Criteria~* Subjects with dementia other than Alzheimer's type~* Subjects with significant
neurological or psychiatric disorders such as stroke, brain tumor, schizophrenia, epilepsy, normal pressure
hydrocephalus, mental retardation, a history of head injury with loss of consciousness, or a history of brain
surgery followed by persistent deficits~* Subjects with allergy to donepezil hydrochloride or piperidine
derivatives~* Subjects with a cause of Alzheimer's disease (AD) which is supported by any laboratory tests such
as Vitamin B12, folate levels, triiodothyronine, free triiodothyronine, thyroxine, thyroid stimulating hormone
(TSH) or serologic test for syphilis
