Main exclusion criteria include:~* Patients with any other cause of dementia shown by MRI findings and
neurological examination in the last 12 months prior to screening visit~* Systemic conditions known to cause
dementia, e.g., hypothyroidism, untreated vitamin B12 or folic acid deficiency, niacin deficiency,
neurosyphilis, HIV infection at screening visit~* Patients with substance-induced dementia at screening visit~*
Patients with Alzheimer's disease with delirium at screening visit~* Patients with severe forms of delusions
(e.g, NPI-12 delusion score of 4 or more) at screening visit~* Patients with evidence of psychosis and/or use
of antipsychotic drugs at screening, or history of significant psychiatric disorder at screening visit
