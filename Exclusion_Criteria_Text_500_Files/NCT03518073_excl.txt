Exclusion Criteria:~* Participants must not have significant neurological disease affecting the nervous system,
other than AD, that affects cognition or may affect completion of the study.~* Participants must not have
serious or unstable illness that could interfere with the analysis of the study or has a life expectancy <24
months.~* Participants must not have history of cancer within the last 5 years with the exception of certain
types of skin, cervical, prostate, and other cancers that are not likely to recur or spread.~* Participants
must not have serious risk for suicide.~* Participants must not have history of drug or alcohol use disorder
within the last 2 years.~* Participants must not have multiple severe drug allergies~* Participants must not
have HIV, Hepatitis B or Hepatitis C~* Participants must not be receiving gamma globulin (IgG) or intravenous
immunoglobulin (IVIG) therapy
