Inclusion Criteria:~* Diagnosis of probable AD (NINDS-ADRDA criteria).~* Age must be 50 years or older.~* Able
to ingest oral medications.~* Caregiver/Study Partner who has direct contact with the participant more than 2
days per week to accompany participant to all visits.~* MMSE score between 14 and 26 (inclusive).~* Modified
Hachinski score of less than or equal to 4.~* Able to abstain from ingesting large quantities of
resveratrol-containing foods (including red wine). 1-2 glasses of red wine or red grape juice daily acceptable;

dietary supplements containing resveratrol.~
