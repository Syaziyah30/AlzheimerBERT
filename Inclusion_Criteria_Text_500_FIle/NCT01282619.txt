Inclusion Criteria:~1. Male/female patient aged between 50 and 85~2. Clinical diagnosis of mild to moderate
probable AD based on DSM-IV and the NINCDS-ADRDA criteria~3. Middle school or above: 11 ≤MMSE ≤ 24, elementary

disease;~5. Hachinski Ischemic Score < 4~6. Hamilton Depression Scale ≤10~7. Informed consent of the patient
