Inclusion criteria:~* Subjects and their caregivers must provide informed consent prior to study entry.~*
Subjects must have a clinical diagnosis of probable mild-to-moderate Alzheimer's disease with a documented
6-month history of AD symptoms~* Subjects must have a regular caregiver who is willing to attend visits,
oversee the subject's compliance with the study and report on the subject's status.~* Female subjects of
child-bearing potential must agree to pregnancy testing and approved form of birth control.~
