Inclusion Criteria:~* Outpatients with a diagnosis of mild-to-moderate Alzheimer's disease according to the
National Institute of Neurological and Communicative Disorders and the Alzheimer's Disease and Related
Disorders Association (NINCDS-ADRDA) criteria (including patients living in residential homes for the elderly

informant to accompany the patient on scheduled visits~
