Inclusion Criteria:~* The subject is a male or female between the ages of 55 and 90 years, inclusive, at
Screening Visit 1.~* The subject meets the NINCDS/ADRDA criteria for probable AD.~* The subject has a
Mini-Mental Status Examination (MMSE) total score of 10 to 24, inclusive, at Screening Visit 1.~* The subject
has a Modified Hachinski Ischemic Scale (MHIS) score of less than or equal to 4 at Screening Visit 1.~* If
female, subject must be postmenopausal for at least two years or surgically sterile~* The subject has an
identified, reliable, caregiver.~
