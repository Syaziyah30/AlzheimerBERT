Inclusion Criteria:~* The patient will satisfy the DSM-IV criteria for dementia of the Alzheimer's type.~* The
patient will satisfy the NINCDS/ADRDA criteria for the clinical diagnosis of probable Alzheimer's disease
(Appendix I).~* The severity of dementia for each patient will be quantified by a Mini-Mental State Examination
(MMSE) score between 10 and 26 (both inclusive).~* The patient is living at home or in a home for the
elderly.~* The patient has a responsible caregiver who is able to provide information about the patient's
functional status.~* Written informed consent is obtained from the patient or the legally accepted
representative.~
