Inclusion Criteria:~* Meets criteria for mild to moderate Alzheimer's disease (AD) with Mini-Mental State
Examination score of 16 through 26 at visit 1~* Modified Hachinski Ischemia Scale score of less than or equal
to 4~* Geriatric Depression Scale score of less than or equal to 6~* A magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) or
computerized tomography (CT) scan in the last 2 years with no findings inconsistent with a diagnosis of AD~* If
female, must be without menstruation for a least 12 consecutive months or have had both ovaries removed.~
