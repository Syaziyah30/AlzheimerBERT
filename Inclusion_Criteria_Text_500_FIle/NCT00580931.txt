Inclusion Criteria:~* Diagnosis of probable AD according to DSM-IV criteria~* Mild to moderate dementia based
on Mini-Mental State Examination \[MMSE\] score between 13-25~* Minimum age 50 years~* Brain imaging (computed
tomographic scan or magnetic resonance image) within 12 months consistent with a diagnosis of probable AD~*
Hachinski Ischemic Score of <4.~* Stable doses of concomitant medications, including cholinesterase inhibitors
(ChEIs) and/or memantine.~
