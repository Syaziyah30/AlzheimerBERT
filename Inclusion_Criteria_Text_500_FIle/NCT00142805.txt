Inclusion Criteria:~* Informed Consent Form signed by patient and caregiver~* Diagnosis of probably Alzheimer's
disease of mild to moderate severity~* Age 50 or older~* If female, 2 years postmenopausal or surgically
sterile~* Hearing, vision, and physical abilities adequate to perform assessments (corrective aids allowed)~*
Caregiver to attend all visits, perform assessments, and supervise administration of study medication~* CT or
MRI within 24 months prior to screening compatible with a diagnosis of probably Alzheimer's disease~* Modified
Hachinski Ischemia Scale score of 4 or less~* ADAS-Cog score between 15 and 35 inclusive at screening~* MMSE
score between 14 and 24 inclusive at screening~* Stable medical condition for 3 consecutive months immediately
prior to baseline~* No clinically significant abnormal findings on the physical examination, medical history,
or clinical laboratory results during screening~
