Inclusion Criteria:~* Part 1: Male and female subjects between the ages of 45 to 65 (inclusive) in good health
with a BMI of 18-32 kg/m2 and negative urine drug screen of abuse test.~* Part 2: Male and female subjects
between the ages of 45 to 85 (inclusive), MRI/CT scans compatible with diagnosis of MCI or early AD, meets the
classification of MCI due to AD as defined by the National Institute on Aging-Alzheimer's Association, and a
BMI of 18-32 kg/m2(inclusive). Subjects must meet the following Cognitive criteria: Subject Memory Complaint,
cognitive impairment in one or more areas, CDR-SB score less than or equal 1.0, MMSE greater than 24, and no
impairment in social or occupational functioning.~
