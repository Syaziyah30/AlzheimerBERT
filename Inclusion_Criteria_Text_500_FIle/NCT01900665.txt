Inclusion Criteria:~* Meets National Institute of Neurological and Communicative Disorders and
Stroke/Alzheimer's Disease and Related Disorders Association (NINCDS/ADRDA) criteria for probable AD~* Has a
Modified Hachinski Ischemia Scale score of less than or equal to 4~* Has a Mini-Mental State Examination (MMSE)
score of 20 through 26 at Screening visit~* Has a Geriatric Depression Scale score of less than or equal to 6
(on the staff-administered short form)~* Has had a magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) or computerized tomography
(CT) scan performed within the past 2 years that has confirmed no findings inconsistent with a diagnosis of
AD~* Has a florbetapir positron emission tomography (PET) scan or cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) result consistent
with the presence of amyloid pathology at screening~
