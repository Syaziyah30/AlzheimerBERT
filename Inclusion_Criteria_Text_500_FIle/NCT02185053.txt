Inclusion Criteria:~* Aged 50 - 79 years inclusive.~* Meeting the diagnosis of probable Alzheimer's Disease~*
Of moderate severity (Mini-Mental Status Exam \[MMSE\] score 10 - 20 inclusive).~* Patients must be in
generally good health as indicated by their medical history and physical examination, vital signs,
electrocardiogram (ECG), and standard laboratory tests.~
