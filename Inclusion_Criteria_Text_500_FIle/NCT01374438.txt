Inclusion Criteria:~1. Male or females 55-85 years of age.~2. Females should be either postmenopausal or
surgically sterilized. Males with female partners of child-bearing potential must use contraception if engaging
in sexual intercourse.~3. Diagnosis of probable Alzheimer's disease based on NIA-AA criteria with MMSE scores
of 20 or greater.~4. Willing and able to take part in up to six study visits over a 5-month period, with the
support of a caregiver as needed.~5. Willing and able to sign an informed consent document indicating
understanding the purpose of and procedures required for the study and willingness to participate in the study,
with the support of a caregiver as needed.~
